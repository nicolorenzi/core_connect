What’s this?

How 1RM is calculated

1RM (one-repetition max) projects the heaviest single-rep effort for a given exercise. Each set estimates its own 1RM using weight × (1 + reps / 30), and the chart highlights the largest estimate recorded on each training day.

Tracking the daily high 1RM helps you see how your peak strength evolves over time.
